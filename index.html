About DJ Tauba

DJ Tauba brings energy, culture, and unforgettable vibes to every event. Specializing in Bollywood, Bhangra, Middle Eastern beats, and everything from pop to house music, DJ Tauba creates the perfect soundtrack for your celebration.

With years of experience performing at weddings, corporate events, and private parties across New York City, DJ Tauba reads the crowd and keeps the dance floor packed all night long.

Proud member of SALGA NYC and founder of Jazbah parties - where culture meets celebration.
